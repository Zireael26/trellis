# ADR 2026-07-10 — GPT-5.6 effort ladder v2: explicit-effort-or-error, exception tiers, ultra lock

**Status:** accepted
**Context.** GPT-5.6 added `max` reasoning effort and `ultra` multi-subagent mode above xhigh, falsifying three doctrine surfaces that said "Codex tops out at xhigh," while recipes hardcoded blanket xhigh (known 009 D8 follow-up). The 5.6 system card reports increased agentic-coding overreach concentrated at highest effort under persistence-heavy prompts; METR reports its highest detected ReAct-harness cheating rate (prompt/scaffold-dependent). Installed surfaces at build: companion v1.0.5 rejects effort >xhigh; codex CLI 0.144.0; catalog advertises max/ultra (ultra listed for Terra too — tier availability is a preflight fact, not press-derived).

**Decision.** (1) Dispatch-time ladder: medium/high/xhigh operating band on `gpt-5.6-sol`; every Codex unit declares effort explicitly — omitted effort is a validation error, never a default. (2) `max`/`ultra` are exception tiers: opt-in per unit, non-empty justification echoed into the dispatch receipt, never a default anywhere. (3) `ultra` is hard-rejected in `.wf.js` recipes until three controls exist: actual-token telemetry, ×4 concurrency accounting, one instrumented run. (4) Tiers a surface rejects fail closed (log + degrade unit to Claude) — never silently clamp. (5) The six-field work-order contract + verbatim honest-reporting clause live in the runnable prompt builders, not just prose. (6) Review of executor output is never delegated to the executor that produced the diff, and never skipped. (7) §2 strength figures stay byte-untouched behind a dated stale-banner until ≥2 independent non-OpenAI evaluations exist (expiry 2026-08-15) — Phase B re-grounds them.

**Consequences.** Effort mis-tiering ends at the validation layer, not by convention; exception tiers are reachable without doctrine edits but leave receipts; the strength table cannot absorb launch-day claims; recipes gain a required-effort input contract every future recipe must honor (tested by an executable matrix against the real recipe files). Cost: dispatch calls that omitted effort now fail loudly and must be updated.
